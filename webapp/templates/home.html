What is Pytheas ?

Pytheas a simple webtool helping you to download data from youtube API, manage and exporting in json.

Try it !

Just put an url from youtube and see what it will respond to you :

How work Pytheas for you ?

  1. Explore : simple view of what youtube share to us as data from a given ressource (video, playlist or channel)
  2. Download : simplified form that allow you to ask data from youtube and download it for you.
  3. Enrich : aggregate your data existing in Pytheas to
